Rungta International Skill University (RISU) R1 is dedicated to providing quality education and practical skill development to students across various disciplines. Our goal is to prepare learners for a successful and innovative future.

About Our University

Established with a vision to create globally competitive professionals, RISU focuses on technical training, research, and entrepreneurial development. We believe in empowering youth with real-world skills and knowledge.

Our mission is to deliver skill-based education and foster innovation through collaboration and continuous learning. We encourage students to dream big and achieve excellence in every field.
